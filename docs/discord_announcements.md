## Announcement 1: The Return (post in #general or #updates)

> **Ingnomia development has resumed.**
>
> Some of you reported bugs, suggested features, and discussed ideas in this server years ago. I've read every single message — all 15,000+ of them across #suggestions, #bug-reports, and #dev-discussion.
>
> Here's what's happened:
>
> **Bug fixes:**
> - Negative thirst crash — fixed
> - Gnomes getting stuck/trapped — fixed, with warnings now
> - UI crashes when scrolling workshops — fixed
> - Save corruption from duplicate kingdom names — fixed
> - Job cancellation not working — fixed
> - Floors randomly disappearing — fixed
> - Rotated workshops not crafting — fixed
> - Game speed lag/freezing — fixed
>
> **New features:**
> - Event/notification log — know what happened and when
> - Trapped gnome detection + alerts
> - Stockpile UX overhaul — auto-accept new items, "for trade" marking, smarter stacking
> - HUD stock counters for food/drink/materials
> - Battle recap after combat
> - Resizable UI windows
>
> Over the next few days, I'll be replying to some of your original messages to let you know your reports and suggestions were heard. Some of you posted these 4-6 years ago. Thank you for caring about this game.
>
> There's a full development roadmap with milestones planned. This is just the beginning.
